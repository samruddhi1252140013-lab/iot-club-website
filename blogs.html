Resume Building Session by IoT Club

Date: 21/09/2025

Category: Career Development | Workshops

The IoT Club, VIT Pune successfully organized a Resume Building Session to help students prepare industry-ready resumes for internships and placements.

The session was conducted by Mr. Dheeraj Rathod, Career Coach, who shared valuable guidance on creating impactful resumes aligned with industry expectations.

  • Resume structure and professional formatting
  • Highlighting technical and soft skills effectively
  • Presenting academic and personal projects
  • Common resume mistakes and how to avoid them

Students from FY, SY, and TY actively participated in the session. The event helped students gain confidence and clarity about career preparation.

FY Orientation 2025 Successfully Conducted by IoT Club

Date: 3 Months Ago

Category: Club Activities | Events
